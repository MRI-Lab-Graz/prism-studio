Dataset Validation

The PRISM Validator ensures your dataset follows the PRISM structure and BIDS standards. It checks for required files, naming conventions, and validates metadata against JSON schemas.

Expected Dataset Structure

  • biometrics/ - Biometric assessments (VO2max, planks)
  • physiological/ - Standard BIDS physio
