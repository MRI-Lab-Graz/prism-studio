Key Highlights

BIDS-first and compatible

PRISM extends BIDS without breaking existing tools like fMRIPrep and standard validators.

Survey modality

Structured questionnaires with sidecar metadata, scoring recipes, and LimeSurvey support.

Biometrics modality

Capture physiological assessments like VO2max, balance tests, or strength measures.

Fast, local validation

Validate large datasets locally with metadata-only uploads and instant feedback.

NeuroBagel-ready

Annotate participants using standardized ontologies and controlled vocabularies.

Local-first by design

All processing runs on your machine—no cloud uploads required.
